FUEL MULTIPLIERS
This script adds Final, Cold Cranking, Throttle tip-in , Ect, and Post Start multipliers to P30 and p72 based roms. It can be applied to Calvin's datalogger 5 rom, and will work with John Que's Boost Tools and Advanced Boost Tools Plus.
