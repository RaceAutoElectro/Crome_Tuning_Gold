FUEL TOOLS 2.0

ABOUT THIS PLUGIN:

Fuel Tools is a CROME plugin which enhances the stock ROM and adds advanced injector sizing capabilities. This allows for propper scaling of fuel tables without the need for further adjustments with regards to differences in injector characteristics and performance.
